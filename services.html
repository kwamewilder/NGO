Our Healthcare Services

Supporting the community with essential medical care and assistance

Monthly Treatment Service

Providing ongoing medical treatment for chronic conditions, including regular check-ups, medication management, and specialized care plans. Our monthly treatment service ensures continuous care for those who need it most.

Monthly Donation: $250

Medical Examination & Tests

Comprehensive medical examinations including blood work, diagnostic imaging, and specialized tests. Early detection and prevention are key to maintaining good health in our community.

Per Examination: $150

Surgical Operations

Supporting essential surgical procedures for those who cannot afford them. Our network of volunteer surgeons and medical facilities helps provide life-changing operations to those in need.

Average Cost: $5,000

Payment of Patients' Debts

Helping patients overcome medical debt through our financial assistance program. We work with healthcare providers to negotiate and settle outstanding medical bills for qualifying individuals.

Average Assistance: $2,000

Medical Supplies

Providing essential medical supplies including medications, mobility aids, and medical equipment to those in need. Our program ensures patients have access to the supplies they need for recovery and ongoing care.

Monthly Supply Kit: $100

Family Sponsorship Program

Supporting families affected by medical crises through comprehensive assistance including housing support, food assistance, and educational resources for children during treatment periods.

Monthly Sponsorship: $500
